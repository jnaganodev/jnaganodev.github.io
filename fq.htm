What is Creatio Hominis?

Creatio Hominis

Is a brand started by Marq Smith as a way to use design and fashion to start meaningful conversations among the youth of today to help create a better future for us all.
In recent years it seems as if the world is ending and the job of preserving what is left falls upon the next generation of young dreamers and artists.
Creatio Hominis aims to use design, fashion, and engineering to help in this initiative and bring light to these issues that we face in hopes to find solutions, and look great while doing so.

How long does Shipping Usually Take?

Shipping
